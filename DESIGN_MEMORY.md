# Design Memory: Hously Media Conversion

## Color Palette Used
- **Primary:** Blue/Indigo (Hously brand colors)
  - Light: `#eff6ff` (indigo-50)
  - Main: `#3b82f6` (blue-500)
  - Deep: `#1d4ed8` (indigo-700)
- **Status Colors:**
  - Running: Indigo/Blue
  - Queued: Amber
  - Completed: Emerald
  - Failed: Red

## Typography Decisions
- **Headings:** Large, bold tracking-tight (Stripe/Linear inspired).
- **Metadata:** Small (text-xs) or extra-small (text-[10px]) for density without clutter.
- **Uppercase Labels:** Used for section headers (e.g., "OUTPUT PRESET") to create clear structure.

## Component Patterns
- **Status Bar:** Persistent horizontal bar at the top of feature pages for background tasks.
- **Comparison Cards:** Side-by-side cards (e.g., Source vs Target) to show transformation value.
- **Rounded Corners:** Consistent 2xl (1rem) or 3xl (1.5rem) radius for a modern, friendly feel.

## Spacing Scale
- **Density:** Balanced/Comfortable.
- **Padding:** Standard 4 (1rem) or 6 (1.5rem) for main containers.
- **Gaps:** 2 (0.5rem) or 3 (0.75rem) between related elements.
